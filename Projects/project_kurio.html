Kurio

Kurio is a conceptual scandinavian furniture store that embodies the core values of simplicity, functionality, and timeless elegance. This project envisions a digital storefront that reflects the curated and minimal nature of Kurio’s offerings. The design emphasizes a calm and intuitive user experience, with thoughtful navigation, harmonious visuals, and a clear presentation of products. The goal is to establish a strong brand identity while delivering a seamless and inspiring online shopping journey.
