Rituals

This project was developed as part of a university design course, with the goal of redesigning the Rituals website — a brand known for its luxurious home and body care products inspired by ancient traditions, focusing on improving navigation, content organization, and visual consistency. The proposed redesign aims to deliver a more modern and elegant experience, featuring a clean and organized aesthetic.
