Chair Short Film

When Michael comes across a mysterious chair, he embarks on an obsession that distorts his perception of reality. CHAIR is a short film created as part of a school assignment, developed under the challenge of working with minimal equipment and resources. The project explores storytelling through simplicity, using limited tools to focus on atmosphere, framing, and emotion.
