ESAD.CR Event

The Events App was developed at the School of Arts and Design in Caldas da Rainha (ESAD.CR) as part of the Fundamentals of Interaction course in the Multimedia Design program. The goal of this academic project was to create a mobile application that streamlines the organisation and participation in school events, enabling students and teachers to explore, create, and manage events with ease.
